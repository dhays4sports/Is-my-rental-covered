Here’s Where Most Landlords Get Caught Off Guard

Most rental owners do not discover coverage gaps until a real claim forces the issue. These are the areas that tend to create the biggest surprises.

Loss of Rental Income

Your policy may not fully protect lost rent if the property becomes unlivable after a covered loss.

Water Damage Limits

Water damage is often capped lower than landlords expect, especially on weaker policies.

Liability Exposure

Low liability limits can become expensive fast if a tenant or guest is injured on the property.

Wrong Policy Type

Some owners still have a homeowners-style setup instead of a true landlord policy.
